The Equity Picture

Nvidia RSUs in Texas:
The Full Tax Picture

💰
State Income Tax: Zero

Texas has no state income tax. California's top rate is 13.3%. On a Nvidia engineer vest of $300K in RSUs, you keep $39,000 more in Texas than in California. On an $800K vest year (principal engineer / architect), you keep $100,000+ more. At Nvidia's current growth trajectory, vest values have been among the highest in the industry — the Texas advantage compounds accordingly.

📈
Capital Gains: Nvidia's Run

California taxes long-term capital gains as ordinary income at up to 13.3%. Texas has no state capital gains tax. Nvidia's share price appreciation over recent years has been substantial. For engineers who hold NVDA shares after vest, the capital gains tax delta between California and Texas can be significant — especially for early employees or those with longer tenures.

🏠
Property Tax Reality

Texas property taxes run 1.8%–2.4% of assessed value — significantly higher than California's Prop 13-capped rates. On a $700K Lakeway home: ~$12,600–$16,800/year. This partially offsets income tax savings, but the net financial benefit at Nvidia compensation levels remains strongly positive — typically $50,000–$80,000/year net after accounting for property taxes.

How much will I save on taxes moving from California to Texas for Nvidia?

Does Nvidia offer relocation assistance for Austin moves?

Nvidia offers relocation packages for qualifying hires and transfers. Packages vary by level and role — confirm specifics with Nvidia HR. If you have temporary housing covered, use that time to rent in Cedar Park or the Domain before buying. The northwest Austin market moves quickly at $400K–$700K; having 3–6 months to search properly is an advantage.

Is the Austin housing market risky — could prices fall like 2022?

Austin prices corrected 15–20% from the 2022 peak and have since stabilized. The northwest Austin corridor is a mature tech-employee market with strong school districts, employment base, and limited inventory relative to demand. Coming from Silicon Valley prices, Austin at any correction level is still a fundamentally different market.

How do Eanes ISD and Leander ISD compare to Palo Alto Unified or Los Gatos?

Eanes ISD (Westlake Hills) is the closest Austin equivalent to Palo Alto Unified — small, elite, well-funded, with Westlake HS in Texas's top 5.
