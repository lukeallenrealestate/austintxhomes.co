The District

About Austin ISD

Austin ISD covers central Austin and is the district most people think of when they imagine living "in Austin." It includes some of the city's most desirable neighborhoods — Tarrytown, Hyde Park, Zilker, Bouldin Creek, Travis Heights, Mueller, Crestview, and large swaths of East Austin. Home prices range from roughly $250K for condos and smaller East Austin homes to $3M+ for Tarrytown estates and waterfront properties along Lady Bird Lake.

The district operates a mix of neighborhood schools, magnet programs, and transfer options. School quality varies significantly by campus, which is exactly why working with a realtor who knows the district matters. A few blocks can mean the difference between a top-rated elementary school and one that is less competitive — and that difference directly affects home values.

Austin ISD is where most first-time buyers, young professionals, and families who want an urban lifestyle start their search. It is also where long-time Austinites hold properties they never sell.
