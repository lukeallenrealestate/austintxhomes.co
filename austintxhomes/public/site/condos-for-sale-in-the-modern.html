About the Building

Austin's Most Anticipated
New Address

The Modern Austin at 610 Davis St is downtown Austin's most exciting new luxury residential tower. Completed in 2025, this 56-story glass tower rises above the beloved Rainey Street Historic District — just steps from Lady Bird Lake, the Hike & Bike Trail, and Austin's most vibrant restaurant and bar scene.

Developed by Urbanspace Real Estate + Interiors and designed by Nelsen Partners Architects, the Modern offers 319 residences with floor plans ranging from one-bedroom condos to full-floor penthouses with terraces. Every unit features high-end finishes, floor-to-ceiling windows, and sweeping views of the Austin skyline or Lady Bird Lake.

At 658 feet, the Modern is one of the tallest residential buildings in Texas — and with its Rainey Street address, it offers the most walkable luxury living in all of Austin.
