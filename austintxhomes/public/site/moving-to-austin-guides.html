Why Austin? The Short Version

Texas Has No
State Income Tax

That single fact drives more relocation decisions than anything else. If you're earning $150K and moving from California, you're looking at roughly $14,000/year in annual savings. From Oregon, $12,000. From New York, $10,000. From Arizona, $3,750. The math is different depending on where you're from — and the property tax trade-off (Texas runs 1.8–2.4%) affects the final number at every income level.

But taxes are only part of the story. Austin's tech job density — Apple, Google, Meta, Nvidia, Oracle HQ, Tesla HQ, Dell HQ, Amazon — is now comparable to Seattle or San Jose. Barton Springs is swimmable year-round. 250+ live music venues. 300 sunny days. The Hill Country is 45 minutes away. Most transplants report a meaningful improvement in quality of life within six months of the move.
