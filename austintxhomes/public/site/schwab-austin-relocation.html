A Different Austin Geography

Schwab's Austin campus sits in the SW Austin corridor — a completely different part of the city from where most Austin tech employer guides point you. Apple, Amazon, Meta, and Nvidia are all in the Domain/NW Austin cluster. Schwab Austin is southwest, which means the entire housing equation changes.

For Schwab employees, the neighborhoods that make sense are Westlake Hills, Barton Hills, South Austin, Bee Cave, and Lakeway — not Cedar Park, Round Rock, or the NW suburbs that fill most Austin employer relocation guides. The SW Austin corridor has some of the city's most sought-after real estate: Westlake Hills sits in Eanes ISD (Texas's top-ranked school district) and offers Hill Country character within 20 minutes of downtown Austin.

This is Schwab employees' structural advantage over employees at Domain-area companies: the SW Austin corridor puts you near better neighborhoods and Texas's best public schools, rather than in the generic NW suburban sprawl that most tech employer relocation guides recommend.

The SW Austin Difference

Domain-area employees (Apple, Amazon, Meta, Nvidia) who want Eanes ISD schools have a 30–45 min commute against traffic. Schwab Austin employees working in the SW corridor have Westlake Hills — and Eanes ISD — essentially at their doorstep. It's the best school district in Texas, and for Schwab employees it's the closest option, not a compromise.

The Financial Picture

Schwab RSUs & Equity
in Texas

💰
Zero State Income Tax on All Comp

Texas has no state income tax on salary, bonuses, commissions, or RSU vesting events. California taxes all of these at up to 13.3%. For Schwab employees with Schwab RSU or stock award grants vesting annually, moving to Texas before a large vest saves the full California rate on that vest — permanently, for every subsequent year in Texas. Document your domicile break cleanly and the savings are yours from the first paycheck.

📈
Production Bonuses Are Untaxed at State Level

Schwab financial advisors earn significant variable compensation through production bonuses and incentive programs. In California, every dollar of bonus income is taxed as ordinary income at up to 13.3%. In Texas, there is no state income tax on any form of income — salary, bonus, commissions, or incentive pay. For a senior financial advisor in a strong year at $280K total comp, the Texas advantage on variable pay alone can exceed $20,000.

🏠
Homestead Exemption & Property Tax

Texas property taxes run 1.8%–2.4% of assessed value in the Austin metro — higher than California's Prop 13-protected rates for longtime owners, but significantly lower in absolute dollars than comparable California homes. File your homestead exemption after January 1 of your first full year of ownership — it reduces your assessed value by $100,000 for school district taxes and caps future annual increases at 10%. Westlake Hills property taxes on a $1.1M home run roughly $19,800–$26,400/year.

The SW Austin corridor gives Schwab employees access to Texas's most prestigious public school district — with no commute penalty. This is the clearest school advantage of any employer location in Austin.

Texas #1 · Most Sought
Eanes ISD — Westlake Hills
8–15 min from Schwab Austin campus

Westlake High School is consistently ranked Texas's top public high school — the Austin equivalent of Palo Alto High School or Gunn High in the Bay Area. Eanes ISD has outstanding elementary campuses throughout Westlake Hills, including Eanes Elementary and Barton Creek Elementary. For families relocating from Bay Area private schools or Palo Alto Unified, this is the closest direct equivalent — at public school tuition.

Western Corridor
Lake Travis ISD
Bee Cave, Lakeway — 15–25 min from Schwab

A well-regarded district for the Bee Cave and Lakeway corridor west of Schwab's campus. Lake Travis HS has strong academics and a competitive athletics program. Homes in this district run $450K–$900K — significantly more affordable than Eanes ISD territory. For families who want the Hill Country lifestyle and newer construction at lower prices, Lake Travis ISD is the compelling alternative to Westlake Hills.

Central Austin
Austin ISD — South/Central Campuses
Barton Hills, South Austin, Travis Heights

AISD is variable by campus — research by specific address. The Barton Hills Elementary and Travis Heights zones have well-regarded campuses. Bowie HS serves South Austin and is the strongest AISD high school option for Schwab employees in the Barton Hills / Bouldin Creek area. For buyers prioritizing Austin character over school district prestige, central AISD zones are worth researching by address.

Private Options
Central Austin Private Schools
St. Andrew's, St. Michael's, Regents

For Schwab families accustomed to Bay Area private schools: St. Andrew's Episcopal School (K–12, nationally recognized), St. Michael's Catholic Academy, and Regents School of Austin are the top options. At senior Schwab comp levels, the income tax savings more than offset private tuition while buying a home that would cost 3x as much in the Bay Area.
