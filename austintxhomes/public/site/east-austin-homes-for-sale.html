East Austin Buying Guide

Buying a Home in
East Austin, TX

Sub-area matters as much as price in East Austin. Two East Austin homes at the same price can be very different investments depending on which sub-area they are in. East 6th core homes have the highest STR demand but also the highest noise and activity. Holly homes near Lady Bird Lake have trail access and a slightly more residential feel. Govalle/Johnson Terrace homes are more affordable but in an earlier stage of appreciation. Chestnut/MLK corridor homes offer a quieter residential block feel at a mid-range price. Luke Allen maps which sub-area fits a buyer’s lifestyle and investment profile before they start making offers — so buyers are choosing the right sub-area, not just the nearest available listing. See the East Austin market report for current sub-area pricing data.

Every East Austin buyer who has STR interest should answer one question before going under contract: can this specific home be a short-term rental? East Austin STR permits are available for owner-occupied properties (Type 1 STR). City of Austin limits non-owner-occupied STRs in 78702 (Type 2), and supply is constrained. Buyers who intend to STR need to verify permit availability before closing — not after. Luke Allen helps every East Austin buyer who has STR interest navigate the permit situation and model the income before they are under contract. A well-located East Austin home can generate $40K–$80K per year in STR income during SXSW, ACL, Formula 1, and baseline East 6th demand. That income potential should be underwritten, not assumed. Read more on the East Austin Realtor page about STR investment advisory.

The spread between a 1940s original-condition bungalow ($500K) and a brand-new spec townhome ($850K+) on the same East Austin block is real — but so are the differences in carrying costs, renovation risk, and buyer pool at resale. Bungalows offer renovation upside; new construction offers lower maintenance and predictable costs. The choice is not obvious and depends on a buyer’s timeline, risk tolerance, and end goals. Luke Allen helps buyers model both scenarios before committing — including renovation cost estimates, resale comps for finished products, and new construction pricing relative to what equivalent finished bungalows trade for on the same street. East Austin school assignments vary significantly by address and have changed multiple times as AISD redraws boundaries. Elementary schools serving the area include Govalle, Sanchez, and Maplewood. Kealing Middle School serves most of the area. LBJ or Reagan High School serves most East Austin addresses. The same street can have different school assignments depending on house number.
