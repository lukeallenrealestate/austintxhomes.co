About Round Rock

Austin's Best Suburban Value

Round Rock is the largest suburb of Austin — a city of 130,000+ sitting 20 miles north of downtown along the I-35 corridor. It's home to Dell Technologies' global headquarters, the Round Rock Express (AAA baseball), and one of the highest-rated public school districts in the Austin metro area.

For homebuyers, Round Rock offers something Austin proper can't: newer homes, bigger lots, better schools per dollar, and real master-planned communities with actual amenities. A $500K home in Round Rock is a 2,800–3,200 sqft 4-bedroom with a yard. The same budget in central Austin gets you a 1,400 sqft bungalow from 1955.

The tradeoff is distance. Downtown Austin is 25–35 minutes depending on traffic. The Domain and Apple campus are 15–20 minutes. Dell HQ is 5–15 minutes from most Round Rock neighborhoods — making Round Rock the natural choice for Dell employees and, increasingly, remote workers who want more house for less money.

This guide covers every major Round Rock neighborhood, the schools that serve them, and the honest tradeoffs between each. Use it to narrow down where you want to focus your search.

Common Questions

Round Rock FAQ

Is Round Rock a good place to live?
Round Rock consistently ranks as one of the best Austin suburbs. Strong Round Rock ISD schools, Dell HQ economic base, newer housing stock than central Austin, and 20–30 minute commutes to downtown Austin and the Domain. Median home price is roughly half of central Austin neighborhoods.
What school district is Round Rock in?
Most of Round Rock is in Round Rock ISD — one of the largest and highest-rated districts in the Austin metro. A few western neighborhoods (like Mayfield Ranch) fall into Leander ISD instead. Always verify the specific attendance zone before buying.
What are the best Round Rock neighborhoods?
For top schools: Behrens Ranch, Brushy Creek, Cat Hollow, Stone Canyon (all feed Round Rock High). For newer master-planned communities: Teravista, Paloma Lake, Sonoma. For established with mature trees: Forest Creek. For Leander ISD: Mayfield Ranch.
How much are homes in Round Rock?
Round Rock home prices range from roughly $350K for smaller established homes to $800K+ for larger Behrens Ranch or Stone Canyon homes. Median is approximately $475–$525K, depending on neighborhood. That's about 50% of what comparable homes cost in central Austin.
How long is the commute from Round Rock to Austin?
Depends on destination. Downtown Austin: 25–35 minutes via I-35 or 130 toll. The Domain / Apple campus: 18–25 minutes. Dell HQ: 5–15 minutes (most Round Rock neighborhoods are within 15 minutes). Traffic on I-35 is the main variable — the 130 toll road is much more reliable.
Is Round Rock ISD or Leander ISD better?
Both are strong. Leander ISD has historically had slightly higher TEA ratings and test scores, particularly at the high school level (Rouse High is consistently ranked top 20 in Texas). Round Rock ISD is much larger with more neighborhood options. For most Round Rock buyers, school choice is driven by which neighborhood fits their budget and commute, not district rankings.
