About the Building

Austin's Most Iconic
Luxury High-Rise

The Austonian at 200 Congress Ave is one of the most coveted addresses in all of Texas. Completed in 2010, this 56-story glass tower stands as downtown Austin's tallest all-residential building — offering 188 private residences with unobstructed panoramic views of Lady Bird Lake, the Texas Hill Country, and the Austin skyline.

Designed with floor-to-ceiling glass walls, private terraces on select units, and interiors finished to hotel-level standards, the Austonian is in a class apart from every other building in Austin. Residences range from single-level one-bedroom condos to full-floor penthouses, giving buyers options from the upper $500Ks well into the multi-millions.

Location is equally unmatched. Residents walk to the Hike & Bike Trail around Lady Bird Lake, to the best restaurants on 2nd and 3rd Street, to Austin's performing arts venues, and to the State Capitol — all without a car.
