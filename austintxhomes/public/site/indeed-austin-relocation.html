Northwest Austin's Tech Corridor

Indeed HQ Is
Northwest Austin

Indeed's Austin campus sits in the Arboretum area near US-183 — the same northwest Austin tech corridor that houses Apple, IBM, Samsung, and dozens of other tech companies. This is one of Austin's most established tech neighborhoods. The Domain is minutes away. Central Austin is 15–20 minutes south on MoPac.

Here's what makes Indeed different from other Austin tech campuses: this is the headquarters, not a satellite. The full range of roles — engineering, product, sales, operations — are here. Whether you're a software engineer from San Francisco or a sales hire from Chicago, the northwest Austin corridor has the neighborhoods, schools, and commute times you need.

Austin's Own

Indeed was founded in 2004 and has been headquartered in Austin ever since. Unlike many Austin tech campuses that arrived in the 2010s–2020s relocation wave, Indeed grew up here. That roots the company culture in Austin in a way that shows — and it means the surrounding neighborhoods have matured alongside the company.

For Families

Austin Area Schools
Near Indeed's Campus Corridor

Austin ISD

Great Hills, Domain area, Central Austin

Northwest AISD campuses — Great Hills Elementary, Anderson HS — are consistently among the best in the district. However, AISD is highly variable; research by specific campus, not district averages. In the Great Hills/Balcones area, the campuses are genuinely strong.

Leander ISD

Cedar Park, Leander

One of the top-rated large school districts in Texas. Vista Ridge HS, Cedar Park HS, Rouse HS all excellent — strong athletics and academics. The #1 reason families choose Cedar Park over staying closer to campus. Leander ISD alone justifies the 183A commute.

Lake Travis ISD

Lakeway, Bee Cave

Small, tight-knit, outstanding. Lake Travis HS consistently ranks in Texas's top 10%. The premium you pay for Lakeway homes reflects this district. Worth it for families with the budget — one of the best school districts in the state.

Round Rock ISD

Round Rock, Pflugerville

Accessible via US-183 north — a solid option if you're considering Round Rock or Pflugerville. Consistently ranked among the best large districts in Central Texas with strong programs across the board.

The Financial Upside

What Texas Taxes Mean
for Your Indeed Salary

💰

No State Income Tax

Texas has zero state income tax. On a $150K Indeed salary, that's $7,500–$12,000 more take-home per year vs. California or New York. Over a 5-year career at Indeed, that's $37,500–$60,000 in compounding savings — before accounting for any equity vesting.

🏠

Property Tax Reality

Texas property taxes run 1.8%–2.4% of assessed value. On a $500K Cedar Park home, budget $9,000–$12,000/year ($750–$1,000/mo). Factor this into your mortgage math — lenders will include it in your DTI calculation. The income tax savings typically more than offset it at Indeed salary ranges.

📋

Indeed RSU & Bonus Taxation

Indeed stock awards and bonuses vest as ordinary income for federal purposes — Texas adds zero state tax on top. Unlike California at 13.3% or New York at 10.9%, every vest in Texas goes further. On a $40K vest event, you keep an extra $5,000–$6,500 vs. California.

Before You Arrive

Austin Relocation
Checklist for Indeed Employees

  • Get Texas driver's license within 90 days of establishing residency
  • Register vehicle in Texas (no annual inspection required post-2025)
  • Register to vote in Travis or Williamson County depending on where you live
  • Open a local bank account — Frost Bank, Amplify CU, or UFCU are Austin staples
  • Set up Austin Energy or Pedernales Electric depending on your address
  • File homestead exemption after Jan 1 of first full year of ownership
  • Get pre-approved for a mortgage before your Indeed start date if buying
  • Research school district boundaries for Cedar Park and Leander — complex boundary lines
  • Download TxTag app if commuting via 183A toll road (essential for Cedar Park/Leander route)
  • Budget for summer electric bills — Austin AC runs May–October, $180–$280/mo
  • Plan for Texas weather: hail insurance riders are common; flooding varies by neighborhood

Common Questions

Indeed Austin
Relocation FAQ

Where do most Indeed Austin employees live?

The northwest Austin corridor dominates. Cedar Park and Leander are the most common suburbs for families — Leander ISD is exceptional and 183A makes the commute fast and predictable. Great Hills/Balcones is the closest established neighborhood with top schools. Domain and Arboretum-area condos attract singles and couples who want to walk or bike to work. Central Austin is popular for employees who prioritize Austin culture and can afford the premium.

How long is the commute from Austin suburbs to Indeed's campus?

Domain/Arboretum: 5–12 min. Great Hills: 8–15 min. Cedar Park: 15–22 min via 183A. Leander: 20–30 min via 183A. Central Austin/Zilker: 15–25 min via MoPac south. Lakeway/Bee Cave: 25–38 min via 620/2222. Round Rock: 22–35 min via 183 north. Avoid South Austin — 40–60 min against PM traffic on MoPac.

Does Indeed offer relocation assistance for Austin?

Indeed offers relocation packages for many roles, particularly engineering and management. Packages vary by level and team. Moving expense coverage is standard for qualifying roles; senior hires may receive temporary housing assistance. Confirm specifics with Indeed HR — if your package includes temporary housing, use it to rent short-term while you scout neighborhoods before buying.

Should I rent or buy when relocating for Indeed Austin?

If it's your first time in Austin, renting 6–12 months lets you experience the corridor before committing. That said, Domain-area and Cedar Park rentals are competitive — low vacancy, rising rents. If you have a family and have done your research, buying before your start date avoids a second move and locks in today's prices. I can run the rent vs. buy math for your specific situation.

What is Indeed's Austin campus like?

Indeed's Austin campus reflects the company's Austin roots — it's a genuine headquarters, not a satellite office. The full company operates from here. The northwest Austin location means your colleagues are spread across Cedar Park, Leander, Great Hills, and the Domain — you'll have plenty of community no matter where you land.

Are the schools good near Indeed's Austin campus?

Excellent — the northwest Austin corridor has some of Texas's best schools. Leander ISD (Cedar Park, Leander) is consistently one of the top-rated large districts in the state. Lake Travis ISD (Lakeway) is among the best anywhere. Northwest AISD campuses (Great Hills area) are strong. Round Rock ISD is also excellent for those commuting north on 183.
