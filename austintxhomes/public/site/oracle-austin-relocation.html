Know Before You Search

Oracle's Austin Campus:
The Riverside Corridor

Oracle's Austin campus sits along the Riverside Drive corridor in Southeast Austin — on the south shore of Lady Bird Lake, with views of downtown Austin across the water. This location puts Oracle employees within striking distance of South Congress, Travis Heights, Bouldin Creek, and South Austin neighborhoods that are quintessentially Austin in character and completely unavailable to employees at Apple, Dell, or IBM further north.

For Oracle employees relocating from the Bay Area, this matters: the neighborhoods closest to Oracle's campus are the most "San Francisco-like" in Austin — walkable, dense, full of independent restaurants and coffee shops, with houses that have actual character rather than subdivision uniformity. South Congress and Travis Heights in particular consistently rank among Austin's most desirable neighborhoods.

The tradeoff: those neighborhoods carry Austin's highest price tags. For Oracle engineers at $180K+, they're attainable. For employees earlier in their career, South Austin 78748 and Kyle offer the same commute direction at dramatically lower prices.

Austin ISD — Mueller
Mueller · Windsor Park

Mueller feeds into Blanton Elementary and Kealing Magnet Middle School — among the stronger AISD campuses. Families choosing Mueller over Travis Heights often cite school predictability as a key reason. Worth the extra 5–10 minutes from Oracle campus for buyers with elementary-age kids.

Hays CISD
Kyle · Buda

Rapidly growing district serving Kyle and Buda. Johnson HS and Hays HS serve the area. Ratings are improving as the district adds resources with a growing tax base. Families choosing Kyle/Buda over South Austin often cite new construction value plus a newer, more consistent school system.

Del Valle ISD
Del Valle · Southeast Travis County

Serves the southeastern corridor between Oracle and the airport. Lower-rated than surrounding districts — most Oracle families looking at this corridor prefer to pay the premium for Travis Heights (AISD) or commute to Kyle (Hays CISD) for better school options.

Before You Arrive

Austin Relocation
Checklist for Oracle Employees

  • Get Texas driver's license within 90 days of establishing residency — bring your California license and proof of Texas address
  • Register vehicle in Texas (no annual inspection required post-2025 — one of the first things California transplants notice)
  • Register to vote in Travis County or Hays County depending on where you settle
  • Open a local bank account — Frost Bank, UFCU, or Amplify CU are Austin institutions
  • Set up Austin Energy — the city's electric utility for Austin proper; Bluebonnet if you're in Kyle/Buda area
  • File homestead exemption with Travis CAD or Hays CAD after Jan 1 of your first full year of ownership
  • Get pre-approved for a mortgage before your Oracle start date — Texas lenders move faster than California but you still need 30–45 days to close
  • Drive South Congress, Bouldin Creek, and Travis Heights before deciding — they feel different from South Austin 78748, and the price gap is significant
  • Research Austin ISD campus ratings at the specific elementary level — district averages are misleading
  • Budget for summer electric bills — Austin AC runs May–October, $200–$320/mo (higher than California in summer)
  • Get hail insurance coverage — Austin gets significant hail storms; standard homeowners policies often need a rider
  • Contact Luke Allen 60–90 days before your Oracle start date if buying — Travis Heights inventory moves fast

Common Questions

Oracle Austin
Relocation FAQ

Where is Oracle's Austin campus?

Oracle's Austin campus is located in the Riverside Drive corridor in Southeast Austin — on the south shore of Lady Bird Lake, with downtown Austin visible across the water. This positions Oracle employees within 8–20 minutes of South Congress, Travis Heights, Bouldin Creek, and East Austin — Austin's most desirable urban neighborhoods, all of which are impractical commutes from Apple, Dell, or IBM.

Where do most Oracle Austin employees live?

Oracle employees — many relocated from the Bay Area — disproportionately choose South Congress, Travis Heights, Bouldin Creek, and South Austin. These walkable, characterful Austin neighborhoods are much closer to Oracle's campus than to any other major Austin tech campus. Families tend toward Kyle/Buda or Mueller. East Austin is popular with younger employees who want urban density.

How does Texas compare to California for Oracle employees?

The difference is significant. On a $200K Oracle salary, Texas's zero state income tax saves $18,000–$26,000 per year vs. California. Austin home prices average roughly half of comparable Bay Area properties. The total financial difference — income tax savings plus lower housing costs — often amounts to $50,000–$100,000 per year in effective purchasing power.

Should Oracle employees rent first or buy immediately?

Oracle employees relocating from California often benefit from renting 6–12 months to understand Austin's layout before buying. The South Congress / Bouldin Creek corridor is very different from South Austin 78748 or Kyle — they look close on a map but have very different lifestyles. That said, if you've researched the market and have a clear preference, buying immediately locks in equity in an appreciating corridor.

What is the cost of living near Oracle Austin?

Travis Heights and Bouldin Creek: $550K–$900K to buy. East Austin: $550K–$900K. South Austin 78748: $380K–$580K. Kyle/Buda: $280K–$460K. Mueller: $480K–$750K. All dramatically less than Bay Area equivalents — and Oracle employees gain $18,000–$26,000/year in state income tax savings on a $200K salary.

Are the schools good near Oracle Austin?

Austin ISD serves most of the South Austin and East Austin corridor — campus-level quality varies significantly within the district. Research specific elementary and middle school ratings before buying. Mueller area feeds into stronger AISD campuses. Hays CISD (Kyle/Buda) is growing and improving. Many Oracle families choose Kyle or Mueller specifically for more predictable school quality.
