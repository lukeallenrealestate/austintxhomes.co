Know Before You Search

Samsung's Austin Campus Is
Northeast Austin

Samsung Austin Semiconductor's campus sits at 12100 Samsung Blvd, in Northeast Austin near the US-290 East and SH-130 interchange. This puts it in a completely different part of the city from Apple (northwest), Tesla (southeast), and Dell (north). Most Zillow searches don't account for this — they show you "popular Austin suburbs" that could mean 50 minutes in the wrong direction.

The good news: Northeast Austin is Austin's best-value tech corridor. Pflugerville is minutes away via SH-130. Round Rock is a short toll-road drive north. Mueller, one of Austin's most walkable planned communities, is a direct shot via US-183. You get more house, more school options, and more commute consistency than most Austin tech corridors — at a lower price point.

Austin Area Schools
Near the Samsung Campus Corridor

Austin ISD
Mueller · North Austin · East Austin

Mueller-area campuses are among the stronger AISD elementaries. District-wide ratings vary significantly — research campus-level ratings carefully before buying. Covers most of Austin proper east of MoPac.

Pflugerville ISD
Pflugerville · Dessau area

Solid mid-range district with consistent ratings. Several well-regarded elementary campuses feed into Pflugerville HS and Hendrickson HS. Good value for families — this is the underrated pick in the Samsung corridor.

Round Rock ISD
Round Rock · north Pflugerville

One of the top-rated large districts in Central Texas. Consistently high ratings across campuses — McNeil HS, Round Rock HS, Cedar Ridge HS. The school premium is real; many Samsung families extend the commute to land in RRISD.

Georgetown ISD
Georgetown · Sun City

Growing and improving district. Wolf Ranch and newer communities feed into East View HS. A solid option for buyers in Georgetown, and the SH-130 commute to Samsung is consistent and largely toll-road the whole way.

The Financial Upside

What Texas Taxes Mean
for Your Samsung Salary

💰

No State Income Tax

Texas has zero state income tax. On a $120K Samsung salary, that's $6,000–$9,600 more take-home per year vs. California. For engineers relocating from California or Washington state, this is immediate and meaningful — it shows up in your first paycheck.

🏠

Property Tax Reality

The offset: Texas property taxes run 1.8%–2.4% of assessed value. On a $420K home in Pflugerville, budget $7,560–$10,080/year ($630–$840/mo). Pflugerville and Round Rock rates are in the middle of the Texas range — factor this into your mortgage affordability calculation.

🎯

Homestead Exemption

Texas offers a homestead exemption that reduces your home's taxable value for property tax purposes. File after January 1 of your first full year of ownership. On a $420K home, this can save $800–$1,200/year. Samsung's HR onboarding materials typically don't mention this — but it's money back every year.

Austin Relocation
Checklist for Samsung Employees

  • Get Texas driver's license within 90 days of establishing residency
  • Register vehicle in Texas (no annual inspection required post-2025)

  • Open a local bank account — Frost Bank, UFCU, or Amplify CU are Austin staples
  • Set up Austin Energy or Bluebonnet Electric service depending on your area

  • Get pre-approved for a mortgage before your Samsung start date if buying

Common Questions

Samsung Austin
Relocation FAQ

Where do most Samsung Austin employees live?

The most common areas are Pflugerville, Round Rock, Mueller, and North Austin. Pflugerville is the closest and most popular — SH-130 south puts you at the campus in 10–18 minutes. Round Rock is a close second, especially for families who want Round Rock ISD schools. Mueller is the urban Austin option with a 15–22 minute commute via US-183.

How long is the commute from Austin suburbs to Samsung's campus?

Pflugerville: 10–18 min via SH-130. Round Rock: 18–28 min via SH-130 or I-35. Mueller/North Austin: 15–22 min via US-183. Georgetown: 28–40 min via SH-130 north. East Austin: 18–25 min via US-290 W. Avoid Cedar Park, Leander, and West Austin — those commutes run 50–65 min in the wrong direction.

Does Samsung offer relocation assistance for Austin?

Samsung Austin Semiconductor typically provides relocation packages for new hires, especially for engineering and technical roles. Package details vary by level and role classification. Confirm specifics with Samsung HR — if your package includes temporary housing, use it to rent short-term while evaluating neighborhoods before buying.

Should I rent or buy when relocating for Samsung Austin?

Pflugerville and Round Rock rentals are more reasonably priced than the northwest Austin corridor, so renting first is a viable option. That said, if you have a family and have done your neighborhood research, buying immediately builds equity and avoids a second move. I can run the specific rent vs. buy math for your situation.

What is the cost of living near Samsung Austin?

The northeast Austin corridor offers Austin's best value. Pflugerville: $320K–$500K to buy, $1,200–$1,600/mo rent. Round Rock: $360K–$560K to buy, $1,350–$1,800/mo rent. Mueller: $480K–$750K to buy, $1,700–$2,300/mo rent. The no-state-income-tax benefit combined with these prices makes Samsung Austin one of the most financially advantageous tech relocations in the country.

Are the schools good near Samsung Austin?

Yes. Round Rock ISD (Round Rock, north Pflugerville) is one of the top-rated large districts in Central Texas. Pflugerville ISD has solid mid-range ratings with several well-regarded campuses. Georgetown ISD is growing and improving. Austin ISD serves Mueller and North Austin — research campus-level ratings as they vary significantly within the district.
