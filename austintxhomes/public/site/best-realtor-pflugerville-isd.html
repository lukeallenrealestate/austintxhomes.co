The District

About Pflugerville ISD

Pflugerville ISD serves Pflugerville, Wells Branch, and portions of northeast Austin. The district has grown steadily alongside the city itself, which has transformed from a quiet bedroom community into one of the Austin metro's most dynamic suburban markets. The district operates multiple well-rated elementary, middle, and high school campuses, with newer schools opening to accommodate residential growth in the eastern sections of the district.

Key neighborhoods within Pflugerville ISD include Wells Branch (one of the area's most established communities, with mature trees, walking trails, and easy MoPac access), Blackhawk (a master-planned community with a golf course, community pools, and strong resale value), Falcon Pointe (newer construction with family-oriented amenities), and the older neighborhoods near downtown Pflugerville that offer character and walkability at lower price points. Homes range from roughly $275K to $600K.

For families who want good schools, a strong sense of community, and proximity to north Austin tech employers without the premium of Round Rock or Cedar Park, Pflugerville ISD is a compelling option.
