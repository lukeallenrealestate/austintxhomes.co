The Financial Picture

Salesforce RSUs in Texas:
The Full Tax Picture

💰
State Income Tax: Zero

Texas has no state income tax. Whether your Salesforce comp is salary, commission, or equity, none of it is taxed at the state level. For a senior AE with a $320K year — salary plus commissions plus RSU vest — you keep $36,000–$40,000 more in Texas than in California. Permanently. Every year.

📈
Commission Income: Texas Has an Edge

In California, commissions are taxed as ordinary income at up to 13.3%. In Texas, they're not taxed at the state level at all. For Salesforce AEs with variable comp that can swing $50K–$200K in a strong year, this makes the Texas advantage especially pronounced — your best commission quarters are fully yours.

🏠
Property Tax Reality

Texas property taxes run 1.8%–2.4% of assessed value. On a $750K East Austin home: ~$13,500–$18,000/year. Higher than California's Prop 13-capped rates, but significantly lower in absolute dollar terms than comparable California homes at $3M+. File your homestead exemption after Jan 1 to reduce assessed value.

Common Questions

Salesforce Austin
Relocation FAQ

Where do most Salesforce Austin employees live?

Central Austin: East Austin, Bouldin Creek, Travis Heights, Barton Hills, and Zilker for buyers who want Austin's authentic character. Clarksville and Tarrytown for senior employees who want understated refinement. Hyde Park and Rosedale for those who want AISD's strongest central campuses. Unlike Apple, Amazon, or Meta employees (who cluster in NW suburbs), Salesforce downtown employees live in Austin proper — the neighborhoods Austin is actually known for.

How much will I save on taxes coming from San Francisco to Austin?

At $200K OTE: ~$18,000–$20,000/year. At $320K: ~$36,000–$40,000/year. At $500K: ~$65,000/year. Coming from New York City with combined state + city tax, the effective top rate approaches 14.8%. Texas has zero. These savings are permanent and immediate from your first paycheck.

How long is the commute from central Austin to Salesforce at 1 Congress?

Downtown condos / Rainey St: 0–8 min. Bouldin Creek: 8–15 min. Travis Heights / East Austin: 10–18 min. Barton Hills / Zilker: 12–20 min. Clarksville: 10–18 min. Hyde Park: 15–22 min. These commutes are dramatically better than Cedar Park or Leander (35–50 min, wrong direction for Salesforce downtown).

Should I consider Cedar Park or Round Rock for Salesforce Austin?

Generally no. Cedar Park and Leander are optimized for the Domain/NW corridor — Apple, Amazon, Meta, Nvidia employees. For Salesforce at 1 Congress Plaza, those suburbs add 35–50 minutes of commute on MoPac against northbound traffic. The compelling Austin neighborhoods for Salesforce employees are south and east of downtown — and they're genuinely excellent places to live.

Does Salesforce offer relocation assistance for Austin moves?

Salesforce typically offers relocation packages for qualifying hires and transfers. Packages vary by level and team — confirm with Salesforce HR. If temporary housing is covered, use that time to rent in East Austin or Bouldin Creek and experience the neighborhoods before buying. The central Austin market at $600K–$900K moves faster than most SF transplants expect.

How are Austin schools for central Austin neighborhoods?

AISD is highly variable by campus — research specific addresses rather than the district overall. The Barton Hills, Travis Heights, and Hyde Park AISD zones have genuinely strong elementary campuses. Eanes ISD (Westlake Hills, 15–20 min from downtown) is the most prestigious district in Austin and rivals Palo Alto Unified. Several strong private schools in central Austin serve families willing to pay $18,000–$30,000/year.
