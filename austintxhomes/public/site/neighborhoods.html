North Austin

Tech corridor, great schools, diverse options

North Austin runs from the Domain south to 183, and from Anderson Lane up to Round Rock. It's the heart of Austin's tech sector — Apple, Dell, and dozens of startups are based here. Housing ranges from affordable condos to high-end new construction.
