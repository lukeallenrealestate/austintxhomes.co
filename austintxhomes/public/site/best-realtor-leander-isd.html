The District

About Leander ISD

Leander ISD serves Leander, Cedar Park, and parts of northwest Austin. It is one of the highest-rated and fastest-growing districts in Texas, with strong academic performance, well-funded campuses, and a steady stream of new school construction to keep pace with residential growth. Homes in the district range from roughly $300K for newer starter homes to $1.2M for custom builds in premium communities.

The district includes established neighborhoods like Avery Ranch and Steiner Ranch — which offer mature landscaping, community pools, and proximity to Lake Travis — alongside newer developments like Crystal Falls, Bryson, and Travisso. New construction is a major part of the Leander ISD market, with multiple national and regional builders actively developing in the area. This gives buyers a wide range of options, but also makes it important to have a realtor who knows which communities are delivering real value.

For families who want strong schools, newer homes, and relative affordability compared to Eanes ISD or Lake Travis ISD, Leander ISD is one of the most compelling options in the Austin metro.
