Background

Born in Texas,
Built for Austin

Austin is one of the most dynamic real estate markets in the country — and one of the most misunderstood. The difference between getting a great deal and overpaying, or between a home that fits your life and one that looks good on paper, comes down to local knowledge. Not just knowing which zip codes are popular, but understanding each street, each neighborhood's trajectory, and what a property is actually worth today.

I built my career on that depth of knowledge. I know which Westlake streets have the best Hill Country views and which ones back up to a highway. I know which Mueller blocks are walkable to the best coffee and which South Congress listings are priced on hope. That granular, on-the-ground knowledge is what I bring to every transaction.

How I Work

Honest Advice.
Zero Pressure.

Real estate has a reputation for being pushy. I operate differently. My job isn't to close a deal — it's to make sure you make a decision you'll be proud of in five years. That sometimes means telling you a listing is overpriced, pointing out a problem before you fall in love with a house, or recommending you wait for the right opportunity instead of settling.

When you work with me, you get direct access — not an assistant, not a team member. You get my personal attention from the first conversation through closing day and beyond.
