The District

About Round Rock ISD

Round Rock ISD is one of the most respected large school districts in Texas. It serves Round Rock, parts of north Austin, and portions of Williamson County. The district consistently earns top TEA accountability ratings, and several of its high schools — including Westwood, McNeil, and Round Rock High — are among the highest-rated in the Austin metro area.

Neighborhoods within the district range from established communities like Forest Creek, Brushy Creek, and Sendero Springs to newer developments like Walsh Ranch and Paloma Lake. The district benefits from proximity to Austin's tech employment corridor — Apple, Dell, Samsung, and Amazon all have significant operations nearby — which drives steady demand from relocating professionals and families.

For families who want strong schools, a suburban feel with genuine community character, and a commute to north Austin tech employers, Round Rock ISD is one of the strongest options.
