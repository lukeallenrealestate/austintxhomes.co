The Tax Angle Seattle Transplants Miss

Washington's 7% Capital Gains Tax
vs. Texas Zero

Washington has no state income tax — so if you've been doing the Seattle-to-Austin math, you may have concluded the tax savings are minimal. That calculation changed in 2021. Washington enacted a 7% capital gains tax on gains over $262,000. It was upheld by the Washington Supreme Court in 2023. Texas has zero capital gains tax at the state level. For senior engineers with significant RSU vesting schedules, this is now a serious annual number.

What the WA CGT applies to: Long-term capital gains from stock sales, RSU vests, investment accounts, and business sales. Does NOT apply to: real estate (primary residence is exempt), retirement accounts (401k, IRA), or income from wages and salary. The threshold ($262,000) is indexed to inflation.

Seattle Context

Seattle Public Schools are highly variable — Garfield, Roosevelt, and Ballard HS are strong; other schools less so. The pattern matches AISD's variability. Seattle families often relied on specific feeder patterns or Eastside private options. You'll navigate Austin schools the same way: by specific campus address, not district averages.

Austin ISD — Central

AISD's strongest campuses cluster in Hyde Park, Rosedale, and South Austin. Research by specific campus address. Strong elementaries: Bryker Woods, Casis, Barton Hills, Travis Heights. Strong high schools: McCallum (Hyde Park) and Bowie (South Austin). Variable elsewhere — don't rely on district-level ratings.

Round Rock / Leander ISD

For Amazon Domain employees or NW suburb buyers, Round Rock ISD and Leander ISD are consistently strong — comparable to Northshore SD or Lake Washington SD in the Seattle area. Cedar Park, Round Rock, and Leander offer strong schools with larger homes per dollar. The right trade for Domain-corridor families.

Eanes ISD — Westlake

The most prestigious district in the Austin metro. 15–20 min from downtown, 20–25 min from Domain. Westlake HS ranks in Texas's top 5. The Austin equivalent of Bellevue SD in quality. Homes $900K–$2M+. The right call for Eastside transplants who won't compromise on school district quality and can access the price point.

Common Questions

Seattle to Austin
FAQ

Does moving from Seattle to Austin save on state income taxes?

Washington has no regular state income tax, and neither does Texas — so on salary and wages, the income tax savings are zero. The significant advantage is Washington's 7% capital gains tax (enacted 2021, upheld 2023) on gains over $262,000. For Amazon and Microsoft engineers with heavy RSU vesting, this is now a serious annual number. For those below the threshold, the financial case rests on COL, housing, and lifestyle.

What's the Washington capital gains tax and how does it affect my RSUs?

Washington enacted a 7% CGT on long-term capital gains over $262,000 in 2021; upheld by the WA Supreme Court in 2023. It applies to RSU vests, stock sales, and investment account gains — but NOT to real estate, retirement accounts, or wages. For an L6 Amazon engineer with a $500K year including RSUs, the WA CGT bill is ~$16,660; in Texas, $0. For an L7 at $800K, savings approach $37,660 annually. Consult a CPA on timing your domicile change.

Where do Amazon employees from Seattle live in Austin?

Amazon's Austin office is at the Domain in NW Austin — the same tech corridor as Apple, Meta, and Nvidia. Amazon Seattle transplants typically land in Domain-adjacent neighborhoods: Avery Ranch, North Austin, Cedar Park, or Round Rock. This is the NW suburbs playbook, not central Austin. If you want neighborhood character with a manageable Domain commute, Northwest Hills or Rosedale are the closest central options.

How does Austin housing compare to Seattle?

Austin is meaningfully cheaper at the median, and dramatically cheaper than Bellevue/Eastside. Capitol Hill-equivalent (East Austin) runs $550K–$900K vs. Seattle Capitol Hill at $800K–$1.2M. Bellevue-equivalent (Westlake Hills / Eanes ISD) runs $900K–$2M vs. Bellevue at $1.3M–$2.2M. Texas property tax rates (1.8–2.4%) are higher than King County (~1%), but the absolute bill is lower on a cheaper house.

What's Austin actually like compared to Seattle?

Both cities are tech-heavy, progressive, outdoor-oriented, and music-centric. The differences: Austin is sunnier (300 days vs. 152), hotter in summer (plan for it), more car-dependent, faster-growing, and culturally more Southern/Western. The live music scene is deeper and more integrated into daily life. The hiking — Greenbelt, Barton Springs, Enchanted Rock — is limestone canyon and cedar, not Cascade foothills. Most Seattle transplants adapt quickly and cite outdoor access and sunshine as the biggest wins.

How are Austin schools for Seattle transplants?

Seattle and Austin have the same dynamic: strong schools cluster in specific neighborhoods and require research by campus address. Hyde Park and Rosedale (AISD) compare reasonably to Ballard/Wallingford Seattle. Eanes ISD (Westlake Hills) is the Austin equivalent of Bellevue SD — one of Texas's top districts, Westlake HS consistently in the state's top 5. Round Rock ISD and Leander ISD serve the NW suburb corridor well for Amazon Domain employees.
