The District

About Hays CISD

Hays CISD serves Kyle, Buda, Driftwood, and portions of the San Marcos area south of Austin. It is one of the fastest-growing districts in Texas, driven by the I-35 south corridor's rapid residential development. The district has invested heavily in new school construction to keep pace with growth, and several of its newer campuses are already earning strong TEA accountability ratings.

Homes in Hays CISD range from roughly $250K for newer starter homes and townhomes to $650K for larger custom homes on Hill Country lots in Driftwood. The district is especially popular with first-time buyers and young families who want to be part of the Austin metro at a more accessible price point than districts like Eanes, Lake Travis, or even Leander ISD.
