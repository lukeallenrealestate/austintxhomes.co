The Building

32-Story Luxury
High-Rise Living

Sienna at the Thompson rises 32 stories above downtown Austin, positioned steps from Rainey Street, 6th Street, and Lady Bird Lake. The building sits atop The Thompson Austin — one of the city's premier boutique hotels — which means residents have access to hotel-caliber amenities that most apartment towers can't match.

Units range from efficient studios on the 15th floor all the way to larger two-bedrooms with panoramic skyline views. The Lofts above The Thompson occupy floors 15–20, while Residences at The Thompson on higher floors offer the most expansive views and larger layouts. Both collections share the building's full amenity stack.

For renters looking at Austin's downtown apartment market, Sienna is benchmarked against buildings like The Whitley and 360 Condos — but offers the added value of hotel services and one of the most walkable addresses in the city.

FAQ

Is Sienna at the Thompson pet-friendly?
Yes. Sienna is a pet-friendly community. You'll need to sign a Dog Rider acknowledgement and comply with their pet policy before application approval. Contact the leasing team about restricted breeds. There is a pet spa in the building.
What utilities are included at Sienna?
Utility inclusions vary by unit and floor plan. Water and trash are commonly included in luxury high-rises; electricity is typically billed separately. Contact the leasing team to confirm what's included for specific units currently listed.
How do I schedule a tour?
Fill out the form above and I'll coordinate with you directly — either a personal walk-through or a virtual tour. You can also call the Sienna leasing team at (512) 900-8000 to schedule directly with the building.
Is parking available at Sienna at the Thompson?
Yes, valet parking is available. EV charging stations are also in the building. Parking is billed separately from rent — contact the leasing team for current rates, which can change based on available garage capacity.
What's the typical lease term at Sienna?
Standard leases are 12 months. Shorter-term and longer-term leases may be available depending on inventory. Ask the leasing team about current flexibility — high-floor premium units sometimes have more term optionality.
How close is Sienna to downtown Austin amenities?
501 Brazos St is literally downtown. Rainey Street is a 2-minute walk. Lady Bird Lake trail is 4 minutes. 6th Street is 5 minutes. This is one of the most walkable addresses in Austin — Walk Score 98. You can live here without a car.
