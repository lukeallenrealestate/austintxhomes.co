Know Before You Search

Austin's Tech
Employer Corridors

Austin's employers aren't centralized — they're spread across four distinct corridors. Understanding where your office sits determines your neighborhood options. Buying in the wrong corridor can add 45 min to your commute.

Domain / NW Corridor
Apple · Meta · Amazon · Nvidia · AMD · IBM

Best neighborhoods: Cedar Park, Leander, Domain area, NW Austin (183/620 area)
Downtown / Central
Google · Salesforce · Indeed · Canva · various startups

Best neighborhoods: East Austin, Bouldin Creek, Travis Heights, Clarksville, Hyde Park
East / Giga Texas
Tesla HQ & Gigafactory

Best neighborhoods: East Austin, Del Valle area, Round Rock, Pflugerville (avoids I-35 south)
West / Lake Austin
Oracle HQ · Charles Schwab HQ

Best neighborhoods: Westlake Hills, Lakeway, Bee Cave, Lake Travis area — Eanes ISD
Round Rock / North
Dell HQ · Samsung Taylor (30 min N)

Best neighborhoods: Round Rock, Pflugerville, Georgetown, Taylor-adjacent (for Samsung)
Parmer Lane Tech Strip
IBM · Nvidia · National Instruments · Silicon Labs

Best neighborhoods: NW Austin, Cedar Park, Pflugerville (183 Toll bypass)
