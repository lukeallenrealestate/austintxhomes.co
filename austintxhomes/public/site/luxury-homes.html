Common Questions

Luxury Homes FAQ

What qualifies as a luxury home in Austin?
In Austin, the luxury threshold is generally considered $1M+, though truly high-end properties with premium finishes, acreage, or lakefront access typically start at $2M+. The luxury tier represents the top 5–8% of Austin's market by price.
Where are the most expensive homes in Austin?
Westlake Hills, Tarrytown, Barton Creek (guard-gated), Rollingwood, and lakefront properties along Lake Austin consistently command the highest prices. Homes in these areas regularly trade at $3M–$10M+.
Are there off-market luxury homes available in Austin?
Yes. Many high-end sellers prefer discretion and never publicly list. I work with Austin's top luxury agents and regularly have access to properties before or instead of MLS exposure.
How do luxury home negotiations differ?
They're more complex. Luxury buyers typically have more leverage, but also more conditions — custom inspections (pool, structure, HVAC, roofing, foundation all separately), longer option periods, and sometimes seller financing or bridge loan coordination. The back-and-forth is more nuanced.
How do I know a luxury home is priced accurately?
Luxury comps are thin and require judgment, not just data. I analyze recent sales of comparable finishes, lot size, views, and location — not just square footage. I'll give you an honest opinion on value before you make an offer.
Can you help me sell a luxury home in Austin?
Yes. Selling luxury requires professional photography, targeted marketing to qualified buyers (not mass portals), and an agent with relationships in the luxury tier. Contact me for a private valuation of your property.
