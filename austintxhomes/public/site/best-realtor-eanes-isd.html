The District

About Eanes ISD

Eanes ISD is consistently ranked the top public school district in Texas. The district serves Westlake Hills, Rollingwood, and surrounding unincorporated areas of Travis County west of Austin. Westlake High School — the district's sole high school — regularly places among the top 100 public high schools in the country, with exceptional AP enrollment, college placement, and athletic programs.

The district is geographically compact, covering roughly 8 square miles. This means a tight-knit community, well-funded schools (driven by high property tax revenue from home values in the $900K–$4M+ range), and lower student-to-teacher ratios than most suburban districts. Neighborhoods within Eanes ISD include Barton Creek, Rob Roy, Lost Creek, and the Westlake Hills core — each with distinct character and pricing.

For families relocating to Austin where school quality is the primary driver, Eanes ISD is typically the first and last district they consider. Explore current homes for sale in Eanes ISD, or look at luxury homes across Austin if you want to compare options in other top-tier areas.
