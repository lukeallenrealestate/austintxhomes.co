The District

About Lake Travis ISD

Lake Travis ISD serves Lakeway, Bee Cave, Spicewood, and surrounding communities west of Austin. The district is highly rated — Lake Travis High School consistently ranks among the best public high schools in Texas — and the area offers a Hill Country lifestyle that few other Austin-area districts can match. Homes range from roughly $400K for newer townhomes in Bee Cave to $5M+ for waterfront estates on Lake Travis.

Key communities within the district include Rough Hollow (a master-planned lakeside community with its own marina), Spanish Oaks (an exclusive golf community with large custom homes), and the established neighborhoods of Lakeway proper that sit along Lake Travis. Bee Cave has grown significantly as a retail and residential hub along Highway 71, offering more accessible price points while still feeding into LTISD schools.

For families who want top-tier schools, access to Lake Travis, and a Hill Country setting within commuting distance of Austin, Lake Travis ISD is one of the strongest options in the metro.
